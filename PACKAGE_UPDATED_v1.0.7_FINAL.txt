Step 1: Extract Package
  - Give client the ZIP file
  - Client extracts to: C:\MBAS\ (or any location)

  - Client reads README.txt

================================================================
   📞 SUPPORT & MAINTENANCE
================================================================

Common Issues:

Q: "Currency still shows $ after changing settings"
A: Refresh the browser page (F5) or logout/login again.

Q: "System tray icon not visible"
A: Check bottom-right corner near clock. May be in hidden icons
   (click ^ arrow).

Q: "Auto-logout too frequent"
A: This is intentional for security. Token expires after 2 hours
   of inactivity. Simply login again to continue.

Q: "Need to disable auto-logout"
A: Edit backend/src/core/auth.py and change
   ACCESS_TOKEN_EXPIRE_MINUTES from 120 to 480 (8 hours).

Q: "Prefer CMD window visible"
A: Use START_MBAS.bat instead of START_MBAS_TRAY.bat.
