**Response (Local):**
> "Main samajhta hoon. Dekhen: PKR 29,999 lifetime ke liye sirf PKR 82 per day pehle saal. Aap chai pe zyada kharch karte hain. Aur ye aap ka daily 2 ghante bacha hai - aap ka waqt kitna valuable hai? Plus, stock errors, expired items, bhule hue udhar se PKR 15,000-30,000 monthly loss bach jata hai. Pehle 30 din mein apna paisa wapis aa jata hai."

**Alternative:**
> "Kya PKR 10,500/month for 3 months aasan hoga? Total same, lekin cash flow easy."

**Response (International):**
> "I understand. Look at it this way: $99 for lifetime use is just $0.27 per day for the first year. It saves you at least 2 hours of work each day - what's your time worth? Plus, you avoid $50-100 in losses from stock errors each month. It pays for itself in 30 days."

## 📞 Emergency Troubleshooting

### **If No Sales by Day 5:**

**Diagnose:**
- [ ] Are you visiting enough shops? (Target: 15-20/day local)
- [ ] Are you getting demos? (Should be 30% of visits)
- [ ] Are demos converting? (Should be 10-15%)

**Fix:**
- If low visits: Wake up earlier, extend hours
- If low demos: Improve opening pitch, be more confident
- If low conversions: Record demo, review what's not working
  - Price too high? Offer payment plans
  - Not clear value? Focus on ONE pain point in demo
  - Trust issue? Bring existing customer testimonial

- Offer free trial: "7 din use karen, agar rakh lein toh payment"

- Partner approach: Find one successful shop, use as reference
- Go international: Focus on digital sales if local struggling
